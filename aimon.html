Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic white marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Construction Period: 1632-1648

Architect: Ustad Ahmad Lahori

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Delhi, India. It is a five-story minaret built of red sandstone and marble.

Construction Period: 1193-1368

Architect: Qutb al-Din Aibak
